üìò How Cross-Question Filtering Works

This tool lets you filter survey responses from one question to analyze another question. Perfect for discovering insights like "How many ministers are in a specific age group?" or "What's the job satisfaction among people who attend weekly?"

üìä Example Workflow:
  1. Select Target Question: "What is your role in the church?" (this is what you want to analyze)
  2. Add Filter #1: Age = "25-34" (filter to only this age group)
  3. Add Filter #2: Attendance = "Weekly" (further filter to weekly attendees)
  4. Click Analyze: See how many ministers, deacons, etc. are in the 25-34 age group who attend weekly
üí° Tip: You can add multiple filters from different questions to drill down into very specific segments of your survey data!
